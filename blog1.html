Internet & Smartphones evolution over the years

Smartphones have gradually become one of the most necessary things for people all over the world. With the continuous improvement in technology, we can accomplish possibilities which seemed to be impossible in past times .

With the popularity of internet began to rise around start of 20th century, smartphones started to evolve more. Companies like Nokia, Samsung and Motorola were the first ones to be the popular bodies at that time.

The revolutionary hike that smartphones availability got with people in India was with the arrival of Jio 4g in India. Jio started providing internet and smart phones at the cheapest rates available at the time as compared to other internet service providers. This lead to the ultimate price decrement of data prices and hence internet began accessible to almost everyone.

Internet and smartphones availability to majority of population of India has led to many positive changes in our country. Education has become feasible and spread to almost everyone. Lack of Information exchange gap between communities has reduced to a higher extent. People have started earning online, do businesses online and are achieving their dreams and moving forward in their life ahead. Medical and emergency services specially during Covid were assisted to a great extent with the availability of mobile and internet among people all around the world.
