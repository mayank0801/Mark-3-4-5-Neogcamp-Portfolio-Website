Binge Watching and its Effect

Binge-watching, is a growing pop cultural phenomenon, whereby people watch multiple episodes of a TV program in one sitting. Watching the same shows, web-streaming series again and again.

Binge-viewing reached a new level of recognition when Netflix and other video streaming services not only started provided opportunities to watch multiple episodes of various TV series, but also offered original content.

Binge watching is a frequent activity done mostly during prime time. Companies understand the need and demand of consumers, and offer the services accordingly to attract more customers .

People from different age groups are suffering from the issue ,wherein for the age group of 12-20 , parents should involve and let their children understand the need of control for the persisting problem. Their should be a discussion between teachers and students on regular intervals highlighting the issue. One should try developing new interests and try spending their leisure time there

There should be awareness amongst people themselves first about how to manage their valuable time and prioritise their health ahead of every other aspects of life. People must understand the need and start initiating with little efforts towards quitting the habit , and be more productive in working life as well as a fruitful everyday life.
